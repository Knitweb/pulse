PAR — Pulse Augmented Reality

PAR rewards verified observations of the physical world. A Quest 3/3S, phone or browser recognizes an object; the confirmed, signed, geohash-anchored observation is peer-validated — and only then is PAR minted. Raw camera frames never leave your personal pod; the fabric stores only the signed, float-free record. No premine, no admin mint.

Launch faucet — 150 places per country

Each country gets 150 granted faucet places; everyone after that joins a fair FIFO waitlist. One address per person. No personal data is stored — only your pls1 address, country code and a timestamp.

Observe → validate → mint

A game, explorer or ChemField bounty escrows PLS on an observation (geohash cell + object class). Your device recognizes the object; low-confidence results require your explicit confirmation before anything is woven. Peers re-run the inference on the digest-committed frame and check geohash proximity. On verification the escrow settles — and PAR is minted, bounded by that escrow.
